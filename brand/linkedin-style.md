Voice anchor for every topic post.

## What this voice does

- **Opens with a real-world claim**, often slightly contrarian. No "today
  I want to talk about..." preamble.
- **Pivots with "But here's the thing"** or similar in para 2.
- **Names the problem before the tool.**
- **Has an enterprise/practitioner aside** grounding it for the audience.
- **Ends with a callback line.** Short, declarative, slightly aspirational.

## Topic post shape

1. **Hook** (1 line). A claim or contrarian framing about the topic.
2. **Reframe** (1 short paragraph). What most people get wrong, or what
   surprised you.
3. **3-4 things that stuck** (numbered list). One sentence + one
   supporting sentence per item.
4. **GenAIPros tie-in** (one paragraph). Specific decision the topic
   produced.
5. **Callback line** (one line).
6. **6-10 hashtags.**

## Length

- 1000-1500 chars typical. 800-1800 acceptable.
- 3-5 paragraphs. Whitespace between them matters.
- One emoji max in body.
- One CTA max.

## Things to avoid

- "I'm excited to share..." / "Today I learned..." — student tone.
- Hype words: "blew my mind 🤯", "game changer", "mind = blown".
- Em dashes (sentence breaks instead).
- Three-emoji bullet lists.
- Naming books, authors, courses.
- More than one rhetorical question per post.
- "Today I studied X" — vague. Say what you actually learned.

## GenAIPros tie-in pattern

Always one specific line. Examples:
- "On GenAIPros, this is why I picked MCP for tool integration."
- "Topic 14 lives in DECISIONS.md as #18."
- "This is the call that makes Phase 15 of GenAIPros work."
